About me

Hello there! I'm a recent computer science graduate from ITESM and a software engineer, with particular interst in machine learning and artificial intelligence. My current role is Junior AI Developer at Capital Certainty, where I use my skills in AI/ML and web development to work with clients at enhancing and escalating their products. I'm passionate about learning new things and understanding how things really work. I never say no to a new challenge and pursue them constantly. I'm a firm believer of continuous growth and always striving to be better every day. If you are interested in knowing more about my work, you can check out my work page.

Outside of work, I consider myself to be a well-rounded person and could probably talk about almost anything, though my two main interests are reading and sports. When it comes to reading, I like to alternate between fiction and non-fiction, with my favorite genres being science (and science fiction), neo-noir, and classic literature. If you want to know more about what I read, my Goodreads profile is here. For sports, I am also versatile and have played a wide variety of them growing up, with basketball being my favorite and the one I've played the longest, even being part of the school's team up until high school. Nowadays, I have switched to tennis and powerlifting with some running and hiking here and there. Though I do not play much basketball anymore, I still enjoy watching it and keep up with the NBA. It's also worth mentioning that I also love statistics and metrics that come from sports. I spend some of my free time keeping up with them and trying to find interesting insights.
